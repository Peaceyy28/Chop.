RICE DISHES

Jollof Rice.

20
Prep(Mins)
45
COOK (MINS)
4-6
SERVINGS
Easy
DIFFICULTY
| INGREDIENT
  • 3 cups long grain rice
  • 5 medium tomatoes
  • 3 red ball peppers
  • 2 scotch bonnets
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • Salt, seasoning cubes
| INSTRUCTION
  1. 01 Blend the tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnets, and half the onion into a smooth paste. Set aside.
  2. 02Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Fry the remainding diced onion until translucent and fragrant.
  3. 03 Add the blended pepper mix and fry on medium heat, stirring frequently, until the oil floats to the top about 20-25 minutes.
  4. 04 Add chicken stock, seasoning, and salt. Stir well, then add the washed rice. Mix everything together.
  5. 05 Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 30 minutes, checking and stirring every 10 minutes until rice is cooked through.
