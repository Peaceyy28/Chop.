Rice Dishes

Jollof Rice.

20
Prep (mins)
45
Cook (mins)
4–6
Servings
Easy
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long grain rice
  • 5 medium tomatoes
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 2 scotch bonnets
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt, seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. 01

    Blend the tomatoes, red bell peppers, scotch bonnets, and half the onion into a smooth paste. Set aside.

  2. 02

    Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Fry the remaining diced onion until translucent and fragrant.

  3. 03

    Add the blended pepper mix and fry on medium heat, stirring frequently, until the oil floats to the top — about 20–25 minutes.

  4. 04

    Add chicken stock, seasoning, and salt. Stir well, then add the washed rice. Mix everything together.

  5. 05

    Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 30 minutes, checking and stirring every 10 minutes until rice is cooked through.

The Story

Built by a Nigerian,
for Nigerians.

I grew up watching food be made with love, technique, and stories passed down through generations. But when I looked online, I couldn't find a place that showed it the way it deserved.

So I built one. This is Chop — a place where Nigerian food finally looks as good as it tastes.
